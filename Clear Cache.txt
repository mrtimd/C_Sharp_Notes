__________
Clear Cache
_______

%APPDATA%\Code

Delete the contents of the Cache and CachedData folders.

Restart Visual Studio Code.

_____________

del /F /Q /S "%APPDATA%\Code\Cache\*"
del /F /Q /S "%APPDATA%\Code\CachedData\*"

To clear local storage (e.g., open tabs history):
window.localStorage.clear()

Restart Visual Studio Code.
